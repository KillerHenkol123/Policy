Privacy Policy

PS7 Selling Assistant (“we”, “us”) provides listing tools that connect to your eBay account.

Data we collect

  • eBay OAuth tokens to act on your behalf.
  • Listing data (titles, descriptions, images, prices).
  • Account identifiers (email/phone) you provide in the app.

How we use your data

To create and manage your eBay listings, support you, and secure the service.

Sharing

We share data with service providers strictly to operate the app: eBay APIs, Supabase (storage/db), Stripe (payments), Twilio (SMS), etc.

Retention & deletion

We retain data as long as needed to provide the service. Request deletion via support at support@yourdomain.com. We also process eBay account deletion notifications.

Security

We use HTTPS and restrict access to production systems and keys.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access/delete your data. Contact support@yourdomain.com.
